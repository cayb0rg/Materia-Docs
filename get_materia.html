Getting your own copy of Materia.

To get Materia at your institution jump over to UCF's Technology Transfer site (that's edu speak for "store") and click Ask a Technology Manager (that's edu for "contact us for pricing"). We're still working out all the details of how this works, but we're very motivated to get you using Materia at your institution.
