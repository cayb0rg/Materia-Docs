Get Materia

  • Engage and Inspire

    Game mechanics, story-telling, competition, instant feedback, and beautifully designed interfaces make for great learning experiences.

  • Incredible Statistics

    Learners, teachers, even administrators get access to great statistical tools that help you deliver a rewarding educational experience.

  • Infinitely Expandable

  • Running in 5 Minutes

    We automated everything it takes to get started, you literally can have your own Materia server running in a few easy steps.

A Better Learning Experience

Materia is an learning platform designed to make online learning rich and engaging. Developed at The University of Central Florida, Materia is now available for any institution to use. It comes with a library of widget engines installed, and integrates into your existing environments. Get started today by downloading the source code.
